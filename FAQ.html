FAQ

-Q: What is Van-Ezy? A: Van-Ezy is an app that allows you to report problems in your neighbourhood instantly.

-Q: How does it work? A: Van-Ezy takes the photo and geolocation you upload, identifies the problem you are facing, and contacts the proper government agencies to come fix it for you!

- Q: Do I have to upload a photo and geolocation? A: Yes, without a photo or geolocation we cannot verify the authenticity of the problem and send appropriate response quickly.

-Q: How long do I have to wait for a response? A: Your query is instantly processed on our site but response time will depend on the responding government agency.
